Why should you care?

The feeling of being alone is a challenge many people face in today's society. In particular, many children and young people has experienced loneliness. This can be the children who are bullied, do not have any friends, cannot handle the pressure that both they and the world put on their shoulders, or who do not receive answers to all of their questions in life. If this problem is not dealt with on an early stage, the damage done can be irreparable.
