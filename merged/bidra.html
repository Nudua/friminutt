Everyone needs someone

The world we live in is an interactive and social world where most people want to participate. However, not everyone is welcome. Most children grow up doing just fine, but some faces problems no one should have to face, and least of all children. The feeling of loneliness is a problem with no easy solution.

"I need someone to talk to!"

Whether it is children who are bullied, do not have any friends, or teenagers who cannot handle the percieved pressure they put on their shoulders, everyone needs someone to talk to. We believe that we can be an important piece of the puzzle in the fight against loneliness, and to be a positive force in children's life.
