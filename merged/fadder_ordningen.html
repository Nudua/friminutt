Contributions

We are a volunteer organization working towards a better world where every child has someone to rely on. We are an independent service to children and young people which comes in to play when the school day is over, and your free time begins. All our employees are unpaid, but we want to be able to offer a great diversity of activities where our mentors can include children and young people. We do not want our mentors to have financial expenses as a result of their volunteering, so therefore we are looking for supporters who can assist us, both with grants or with gift certificates, etc., to activities or experiences.
