Frequently Asked Questions

Subheader

How can I sign up?

You can sign in by using Feide. We have chosen Feide because 97% of Norway's elementary school and high school students have a user on Feide. If you do not have the ability to log in by using Feide you can sign up in our app, or contact us at support@friminutt.no.

Does it cost money?

No, Friminutt is a free service. We encourage activities that are free, but we have mentors who sometimes contribute to the cause. This gives us the opportunity to arrange events and activities that would normally cost money. Businesses that would like to contribute to the organization can e-mail us at business@friminutt.no.

Who can participate?

All students in elementary school and high school can participate. It requires a Feide-bruker to sign in, but if your school does not offer this, you can send a e-mail to support@friminutt.no.

Is it anonymous?

The service is not anonymous. You need to login with your Feide user. This is to avoid abuse of the service. It will not be possible for other users to see your profile, and only your mentor will know your identity.

Who are the mentors?

Most mentors are students from colleges and universities. They have very different backgrounds and interests, but one thing they all have in common is that they want to be here for you!

Can I change the assigned mentor?

Mentor that is assigned to you, is based on your interests and hobbies. If you are not satisfied with your mentor, we will help you find a mentor that is right for you.

How can I become a mentor?

To become a mentor you can og to the mentor page "mentor" located under the page "Solution". There you will find information about the requirements to become a mentor. We hope you will join us!

Do my parents get to know what I am asking
about?

No, what you and your mentor are talking about will be confidential, but we save the chat history to be able to ensure that the work we do holds a high quality. This is also done to prevent misuse of the service. Should the information, that a mentor receives, be of such character that it might have an impact on people's life and health, we will contact the parents or the authorities for support. The contents of a chat will never be used for marketing or commercial use.
