Find your mentor today!

Why do we do it?

Children today are experiencing bullying, anxiety, depression and an enormous pressure to live the perfect life. We believe that today's youngsters are the adults of tomorrow, and they deserve to grow up in a world where they are seen and heard.

How do we do it?

Through our amazing mentor representatives, we establish the connection between the mentor and the children.

What do we do?

We create a safe place where they can communicate through chat, and participate in various recreational activities and a place where questions can be asked, or fun can be had.

Question on your mind?

We are happy to answer your questions. Send an email to post@friminutt.no and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
